The Ultimate Delicacy
The Ultimate Delicacy, was founded in October of the year two thousand, to fill a gap in the Internet world. This gap was the need for Scott Moore of NOP Design to test the NetCharge.com software system, RocketPay, and demo the NOP Design FreeCart shopping cart. This site is a simple facade, and there are no products actually for sale. If you place an order on this site, you will receive only a thank you page, and no actual product.

Because Scott believes in only the best for design, this site has been designed to truly represent a typical E-Commerce client, and has all of the typical functions.

All images used on this site were either developed by Scott Moore of NOP Design, or licensed clipart from Corel® corporation.
